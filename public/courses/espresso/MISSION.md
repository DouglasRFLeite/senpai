# Mission: Brewing Better Espresso

## Why
I want to pull café-quality espresso at home every morning without wasting beans on guesswork.

## Success looks like
- Dial in a fresh bag of beans in under five shots
- Consistently hit a 1:2 ratio in 25–30 seconds
- Diagnose a sour or bitter shot from taste alone

## Constraints
- Fifteen minutes of practice per morning

## Out of scope
- Latte art
