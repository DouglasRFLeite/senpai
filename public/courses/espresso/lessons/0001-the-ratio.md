---
title: The Brew Ratio
---

Espresso is defined by a **ratio**: the weight of liquid in the cup divided by the weight
of dry grounds in the basket. A classic shot is *1:2* — 18 grams of coffee yielding 36
grams of espresso.

Weigh both. Guessing by the cup's fill line is the single biggest source of
morning-to-morning inconsistency.

## Check yourself

:::quiz
At a 1:2 ratio, an 18 g dose should yield how much espresso?

- [ ] 27 grams in the cup
- [x] 36 grams in the cup
- [ ] 18 grams in the cup
:::

:::quiz

:::
